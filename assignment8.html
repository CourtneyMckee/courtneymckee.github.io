Circuit

I soldered a red led with a 220 resistor. In order to make this I first put in the resistor and pushed down the sides so that it would stay in place. I then clipped the board and heated up the board and the metal on the resistor. This allowed the lead free solder to heat up and stick to the board.
I then put the red LED in and folded down the sides of the metal so that it would stay in place. I once again went through the process of soldering and clipping the board. When soldering I had to make sure that I wore glasses for safety.
